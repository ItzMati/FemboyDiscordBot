Femboy Guesser Bot

/random {high} {amount}
Gives {amount} random numbers ranging from 0 to {high}

/send_image
Sends a random image or video from the couple of folders of gay things i gave it.

/send_femboy {place}
Sends an image of a femboy from r\femboy. {place} is the feed you want it from (like new, hot, top or rising)

/send_number {number}
This sends back the number you give it.

/send_reddit {reddit} {place}
This sends a picture from the subreddit you provide in the {reddit} parameter. {place} is the feed you want it from (like new, hot, top or rising)

/guess_femboy
Plays the femboy guessing game. It's a game were you have to guess if an image has a femboy or a woman in it.

/guessing_points (member)
Shows the amount of points you have in the femboy guessing game or shows the amount of points someone else has.

/leaderboard
Shows a leaderboard of the Femboy guessing game
